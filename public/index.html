How to Play

  1. Click the "Start" button to begin the game.
  2. Use the left and right arrow keys to move the paddle left and right.
  3. Catch the required number of objects before the timer runs out to move to the next level.
  4. Avoid catching objects that reduce your score (red balls).
  5. Beat all three levels to win the game.
